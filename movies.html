My Favourite Movies of Various Genres

Men in Black (1997)

Secret agents protect Earth from aliens. Cult comedy action movie starring Will Smith and Tommy Lee Jones.

Captain America: The First avenger (2011)

Steve Rogers volunteers to take part in an experiment that will turn him into the super-soldier known as the First Avenger. Rogers joins the United States military along with Bucky Barnes and Peggy Carter to fight the hostile organization HYDRA, run by the ruthless Red Skull.

The Princess Diaries (2001)

Fifteen-year-old Mia lives in San Francisco and leads an ordinary life for an American teenager. But her father dies, and Mia learns that he was a prince of a European country, and she is now the sole heir to the throne. Her grandmother, the Queen, arrives in California to inform Mia of her origins and to see if she will accept the throne. She wants to make Mia a true princess by teaching her etiquette and manners. By her sixteenth birthday, the girl must decide whether to go to Europe to rule the country or stay with her mother in San Francisco, leading the ordinary life of an American teenager...
